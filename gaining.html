Gaining Access

This is where the ethical hacker does the actual hacking. He uses all the infomation obtained and analyzed from the previous two phases to lunch a full fledged attack on the system or network the ethical hacker is trying to infiltrate.

He exploits all the exposed vulnerabilities and gains control of the system he has hacked.

The goal is to assess the security posture comprehensively by simulating potential attacker techniques. The insights gained guide organizations in strengthening their defenses against real-world cyber threats.
